Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A clipboard manager that's powerful enough for developers and simple enough for everyone.

Automatic History

Every copy is captured instantly. Text, images, files, RTF, URLs, and code — all searchable, all organized.

Post-Quantum Encryption

Hybrid X25519 + ML-KEM-768 with AES-256-GCM. Your clips are encrypted before they ever touch disk.

Touch ID

Unlock your history with a fingerprint. Configurable auto-lock keeps your data protected when you step away.

Instant Search

Find anything you've ever copied in milliseconds. Powered by an in-memory inverted index — no slow scans.

Smart Categories

Clips are auto-tagged as text, URL, code, email, path, number, image, or file. Filter to find exactly what you need.

Pin & Notes

Pin important clips to keep them forever. Add notes for extra context. Your clipboard, organized your way.

Security

Encrypted before it
touches the disk.

Every clip is wrapped in hybrid post-quantum encryption. Even if someone copies your database, they get nothing.

1

You copy something

Clipboard Vault captures the content and immediately encrypts it with a fresh ephemeral key pair.

2

Hybrid key exchange

X25519 and ML-KEM-768 run in parallel. Their shared secrets are combined via HKDF to derive a unique AES-256 key.

3

AES-256-GCM seals it

Content is authenticated-encrypted. The ciphertext, nonce, and ephemeral keys are stored. Plaintext is never written.

4

Touch ID to decrypt

Private keys live in macOS Keychain. Only your fingerprint or password can unlock them. Keys are zeroed from memory on lock.
