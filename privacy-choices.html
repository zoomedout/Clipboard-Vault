Your Privacy Choices

You have complete control over your data in Clipboard Vault.

No Choices Needed

Clipboard Vault collects zero personal data, makes zero network requests, and uses zero tracking. There's nothing to opt out of because we never opted you in.

What You Can Control

Delete Individual Items

Right-click any clip in the history panel and choose Delete, or swipe to remove it. Deleted items are permanently erased.

Clear All History

Open Settings within the app and choose "Clear History" to permanently delete all unpinned clipboard items at once.

Auto-Delete by Age

Configure automatic deletion of old clips in Settings. Choose from 1 day, 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, or keep forever.

Disk Space Limits

Set a maximum disk space for stored images and files. When the limit is reached, the oldest unpinned media is automatically removed.

Lock & Authentication

Configure auto-lock delay and choose between Touch ID or password authentication. Lock immediately or after a delay of your choice.

Complete Data Removal

To remove all data permanently, delete the app and remove ~/Library/Application Support/ClipboardVault/

Data We Never Collect

Clipboard Vault does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

❌ Personal information   ❌ Usage analytics   ❌ Crash reports
❌ Device identifiers   ❌ Location data   ❌ Advertising data
❌ Cookies or web tracking   ❌ Any network telemetry
